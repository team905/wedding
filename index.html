What should I wear?

Mehendi: Colorful & festive. Sangeet: Cocktail or Indo-Western. Ceremony: Traditional Indian formal. Reception: Black tie or formal Indian.

Can I bring a plus one?

Please check your invitation. If it says "and guest," you're welcome to bring someone. Include their name in your RSVP.

Are children welcome?

Yes! We'll have a supervised kids' zone with activities during all events.

Will there be dietary options?

Absolutely — vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options available. Mention restrictions in your RSVP.

Can I take photos during the ceremony?

We request an unplugged ceremony — phones away during rituals. Our photographer captures everything. At other events, snap away! Use #ShubhamWedsVaibhavi

By when should I reply?

Please respond by 30th June 2026 so we can finalize catering and seating.

Is parking available?

Yes — complimentary valet parking at the venue entrance for all guests.
